PROJECT DETAILS & UTILITY


- For the past year the Master Sensei’s here at KungFu Koalas have been working hard to develop an NFT that can provide an actual benefit to its owner. With the world in its current state we don’t believe it’s honorable to ask people to support a project that doesn’t bring them any value in the real world.


- Here at KungFu Koalas we’ve always had a passion for sports. From mixed martial arts in the UAE to second division soccer in Croatia we don’t discriminate. Watching all of these can sometimes be a challenge without paying for multiple expensive streaming services or scrolling endless links trying to find a good quality stream.


- We’ve solved this problem by creating a virtual bar in the metaverse called The Bamboo Club.


- The Bamboo club will stream all LIVE sports channels across the world in one place. There will be 200+ screens where you can hang out and socialize with friends whilst watching your chosen sports in the metaverse. By simply owning a KungFu Koala NFT you will gain access to The Bamboo Club Cinema which will show the latest blockbuster movies from across the world.
